## 5. Envelope Liner

**Size:** Trimmed to fit inside A7 envelope flap (approximately 5 x 4.5 inches, cut to match flap shape)
**Orientation:** Landscape (follows flap geometry)
**Background:** Sunset gradient (135deg, coral-light to coral to pink)

### Content

- Scattered cream/white doodle pattern across the entire surface
- Doodles include: small champagne glasses, stars, hearts, Moose silhouette, CN Tower outline
- Doodles are small (approximately 0.25in to 0.5in each), randomly placed, semi-transparent or cream-colored against the gradient
- No text on the envelope liner

### Layout

- Sunset gradient fills the entire liner area, extending to bleed
- Doodle pattern is evenly distributed but not rigidly gridded -- organic, scattered placement
- Density is moderate: enough to create a pattern without feeling cluttered
- All doodles use cream (`#FFF8F0`) stroke color (reversed from the standard navy-on-cream)
